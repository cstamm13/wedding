We hear you like questions...

OMG. I saw they have rooms at the castle! Can I stay there?

We respectfully request you do not try to book a room at the castle. Rooms are limited and we would like to give priority to the wedding party and immediate family. Once we have an idea of how many rooms are left over it will be first come first served. The Sheraton is pretty sweet, and they have been very nice to us. That and there's almost no potential for coming face to face with a possible ghost if one exists perhaps.

OMG. If I'm staying at the hotel, how can I get to the venue?!

We are planning on having a shuttle service to take our guests to and from the venue. If that doesn't work out, Casey will drive up to 3 people at a time in his stylish Hyundai Sonata.

OMG. What do I wear?!

The wedding colors are black and gold so we would respectfully request that guests try their best to wear something other than black or gold. We look forward to seeing everyone dressed for the occasion! The ceremony and reception are in a castle so let that be your guide in choosing your wedding attire.

OMG. Can I bring my kids?

If there’s a butt that needs a chair and a tummy that needs a plate, we respectfully request that you let us know just how many there are, and that goes for guests of all ages. If you’re still unsure and just want to check with one of us, feel free to drop us a line here.

OMG. I’m allergic to - or don’t like - your food options!

Let us know as soon as you possibly can so we can get in touch with the caterers to arrange for an alternative.

OMG. Is the castle really haunted?!

I guess we’ll find out!

OMG. Do you really have four cats?!

Indeed we do! Head on over to the _________ to see their adorable fuzzy faces.

OMG. This site is amazing! How many thousands of dollars did you pay to have it made?

You're so flattering. The truth is that Casey built the site with his own two hands, a few cats, and fleeting thoughts of arson. We didn't like the idea of the generic wedding websites, so we decied to go with something more customizable and more our style.
