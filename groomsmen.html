Jose Valencia

Best Man

Once upon a time, Casey's mom gave him the gift of music by buying him a month's worth of bass lessons. Casey walked in to his first lesson and the rest is basically history...sort of. Long story short, good times ensued. Casey and Jose have played music together for a few years in The Stonecutters, yo' mama's favorite 90's cover band, and have gone on many adventures of which I am not at liberty to speak. It's sort of a "members only" thing. You, dear reader, just wouldn't understand. Anyone who meets Jose will testify that he is a great person and a great friend, though his preference when it comes to the great debate: Backstreet Boys or N*SYNC, his character may be called into question. Ask him about it. He and his wife, Kelly Bennett, embarked on the incredible business journey and opened Valencia Music, which offers the best music instruction in the Greater Westfield area.

Dan Bulich

Groomsman

Casey remembers the first time he ever met Dan. Actually, that’s not true. He remembers almost nothing about meeting Dan, except for the fact that he met him and shortly thereafter we were in a band together. Dan has unmatched knowledge of and an endearing affinity for 1980s movie soundtrack music. Once an aspiring comedian, Dan sought a more lucrative career in music. It was either a purely financial move or at the urging of others because his jokes could really use some work… Dan is a skilled creator of kickass power hours and his video game knowledge is rather impressive. Dan’s two favorite things are commitment and changing himself. He’s an Aquarius, he enjoys Miller Lite and quiet evenings at home watching and re-watching Independence Day to learn every single word of Bill Pullman’s epic speech. You know the one...
