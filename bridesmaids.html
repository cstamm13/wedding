Stephanie McCartney

Matron Of Honor

My memory is a little hazy but I’m told the story goes like this: Stephanie and I first met at a hoppin’ joint in Okinawa, Japan, and while I don’t remember it, I will forever recognize it as the first day of the rest of my life. Surrounded by a bunch of other youngsters similar in age, Stephanie was brought over to where I was hanging out, and she immediately complimented me on my outfit. Stephanie’s chaperones asked her if she knew me, and she said, “Sure I do. That’s Bobbi.” As it turns out, the hoppin’ joint was the hospital where I’d just been born, the chaperones were our parents, and Bobbi wasn’t my name at all. Mispronouncing the word, “baby,” Stephanie coined the moniker by which pretty much everyone knows me today.

Kelly Bennett

Bridesmaid

On a warm June evening, I made the harrowing journey from Brooklyn, New York to Nutley, New Jersey to see a band play. It was the band Casey was in with Kelly. All by myself, I travelled to an unfamiliar land for the love of music (and because I thought the bass player was pretty cute). After the show, I met Kelly, the band’s front woman. I can’t, for the life of me, remember what our first exchange was (possibly because we became instant friends that night, hanging out until the sun came up) but I know it was a sarcastic remark that immediately won Kelly’s heart. Kelly’s Simpsons knowledge is basically the equivalent of my Futurama knowledge, only better. Casey and I were lucky to have been a part of Kelly (and Jose’s) pre-wedding festivities two years ago and I am so incredibly happy they’ve both agreed to be a part of ours!!!

Sam Sutera

Bridesmaid

Sam and I developed a pretty quick friendship despite the fact that she was pretty close to starting off as my arch nemesis as a result of the trivia game we often play. There we were at a bar in New Jersey when the group introduced me to The Wheel. It’s basically impromptu trivia where we each think of questions for one another based on our levels of familiarity with the subject matter. Sam had been told that I was pretty knowledgeable when it comes to Futurama. In our very first verbal interaction with each other after being introduced, Sam stumped me on a complete-the-line question to which I totally should have known the answer. Humbled, I gave her the “good game” handshake and we’ve been friends ever since.

Stephanie Stamm

Bridesmaid

I could start by telling you about how Stephanie and I first met but I think that’s a story much better told in person…after a few drinks and with no young or easily offended ears in range. Who knows? Maybe the story will be told to the entire crowd on the big day. If it is, don’t say I didn’t warn you! Coincidentally, Stephanie and I “met" on her wedding night! Suffice it to say this little nugget of our history will never be forgotten. Something about first impressions… Stephanie is probably the best sister-in-law I could ever hope for and it helps that she shares the same first and middle name with my actual sister. We’ve laughed together, drank many, many beers together, made fun of Casey together, and that’s typically within the first five minutes of being around one another!
