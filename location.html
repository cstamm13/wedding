Skylands Manor

5 Morris Road, Ringwood, New Jersey 07456

Skylands Manor features 100 acres of formal botanical gardens and 1,000 acres of natural woodlands in Ringwood, New Jersey. Designed in the mid-1920's by the distinguished American architect John Russell Pope, the Tudor Revival Mansion was intentionally made to appear centuries old. Both the ceremony and reception will be held within the manor, featuring the finest of food, an open bar, fantastic music, and your two favorite people (who just so happen to be getting married).
